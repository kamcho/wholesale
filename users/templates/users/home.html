Crowdsourced Group Buys

Join or create buying pools with other businesses to combine demand and reach factory minimum order quantities (MOQs). No more missing out on wholesale deals due to small order sizes.

Verified Global Factories

Access audited factories and authorized exporters from 50+ countries. All suppliers are vetted for quality, compliance, and reliability before joining our platform.

Secure Escrow Payments

Payments are held in escrow and released only when milestones are met: production completion, quality inspection, and delivery confirmation. Your money is always protected.
